My Favorite Manga and Anime

Fruits Basket

Genre: Reverse harem, Romantic comedy, supernatural

It is both an anime and manga. The manga was published in 1998-2006. There are 23 volumes. The first anime was release in 2001 and it was unfinished according to the manga with 26 episodes. They released a reboot of the anime in 2019. There is 25 episodes so far. There is also a sequel to the manga called Fruits Basket Another published in 2015-2019. It has 3 volumes.

Fruits Basket is probably my all time favorite anime/manga there is. It is about this girl named Tohru Honda who becomes homeless because her grandfather decided to renovate his house. He asked her if she could live with one of her friends for the time being. She did not want to be a burden to her friends, so she decided to live in a tent. On he way to school she finds this house in the middle of the woods. She finds out that the prince of her school Yuki Sohma lives there with his cousin Shigure Sohma. They find out that Tohru has been living in a tent in the woods and take her in to live with them. She meets Yuki's rival Kyo Sohma. Later on she finds out that if the Sohmas are hugged by the opposite sex they turn into one of the animals from the chinese zodiac. It is a curse that was put on the family a long time ago.

I guess I just have this weird obsession of the zodiacs. I find them really interesting. This anime/manga is just so sweet and covers the stuggles that the Sohmas have to face due to their curse.

Fairy Tail

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy

It is both anime and manga. The manga was published in 2006-2017 with 63 volumes. The anime was released in 2009 and ended in 2019 with a total of 328 episodes. There are two movies. There is a prequil called Fairy Tail Zero with only one volume. It was released in 2014 and it is part of the anime. There is also a sequel called Fairy Tail: 100 Year Quest. It was released in 2018. It has 5 volumes and is still going on right now.

Fairy Tail is about this girl named Lucy Heartfelia who really wants to be appart of the worlds strongest guild called Fairy Tail. She runs into this mysterious traveller named Natsu Dragneel and his cat friend Happy, after he saved her from a love spell that was put on her by a man who claims that her is the famous Salamander of Fairy Tail. Later she finds out that, that man is an imposter and the real Salamander is actually Natsu. Natsu ends up taking Lucy with him to his guild after he distoryed the town where they met. Lucy was able to join the guild. she ends up going on guest and adventures with her new friends in the future.

The reason why I like this anime/manga is because it has a lot of magic and fancey. It is also really funny and there is also the astrological zodiac in it.

Vampire Knight

Genre: Dark fantasy, Romance, Supernatural

It is a manga and anime. The manga was pubished in 2004-2013 with 19 volumes. The anime was released in 2008 with 13 episodes and is unfinished according to the manga. The is a sequal called Vamire Knight Memories that was released in 2016. It has 4 volumes and is still going.

Vampire Knight is about this girl named Yuki Cross who lives her adopted father at Cross Academy. In the past she was attacked by a vampire and was save by Kaname Kuran. Years later She is a night watch guardian for the day class. The day class is full of humans. Where as the Night class is full of vampires. She works along side her friend Zero Kiryu, a boy that her father took in when they were younger. They try to have vampires and humans co-exist.

I like this anime/manga because to be honest I am a sucker for romance. This anime/manga is pretty much a love triangle between humans and vampires. It it pretty much the japanese verion of Twilight, but without werewolves.

Genre: Dark fantasy, Horror, Thriller

It is both anime and manga. The manga was published in 2011-2014 with 14 volumes. The anime was released in 2014 with 12 episodes. There is a sequal called Tokyo Ghoul:re. It is both anime and manga. The manga was published in 2014-2018 with 16 volumes. The anime was released in 2015 with 12 episodes

Tokyo Ghoul is about a guy names Ken Kaneki. He is a guy who is in love with books and does not talk to many people. Then one day he gets asked out on a date by this girl named Rize. She goes to this coffee shop that he goes to. While they ae on the date Kaneki find out the Rize is a ghoul. Ghouls are creaters that can disguise themselves as humans. They eat humans. Ken finds out that she is a ghoul because she tries to eat him. As she is going after him he gets seriously injured. Then she some how get swished by building beams and dies. The ambulance picks both of them up and takes them to hospital. The doctors same Kaneki by replaceing his damaged organs with Rize's organs. Later Kaneki recovers and find out that he had been turned into a half ghoul and can not eat human food anymore. the only thing he can have is coffee and human.

I like this anime/manga because I like how they mix reality and fantasy together. I alsp really like horror.
