About

Genre: Reverse harem, Romantic comedy, supernatural

It is both an anime and manga. The manga was published in 1998-2006. There are 23 volumes. The first anime was release in 2001 and it was unfinished according to the manga with 26 episodes. They released a reboot of the anime in 2019. There is 25 episodes so far. There is also a sequel to the manga called Fruits Basket Another published in 2015-2019. It has 3 volumes.

Summary

It is about this girl named Tohru Honda who becomes homeless because her grandfather decided to renovate his house. He asked her if she could live with one of her friends for the time being. She did not want to be a burden to her friends, so she decided to live in a tent. On he way to school she finds this house in the middle of the woods. She finds out that the prince of her school Yuki Sohma lives there with his cousin Shigure Sohma. They find out that Tohru has been living in a tent in the woods and take her in to live with them. She meets Yuki's rival Kyo Sohma. Later on she finds out that if the Sohmas are hugged by the opposite sex they turn into one of the animals from the chinese zodiac. It is a curse that was put on the family a long time ago.
